Minimum programming requirement for a 
bean is the bean class plus two interfaces.

Shows you how to: 

    Write a simple bean class.
    Write the home and remote interfaces.
    Write and use a batch file to control the compilation process.

Discusses life cycle of a bean and the callback 
methods that the bean uses to monitor the life cycle.

Discusses session context, bean creation, and the ejb package

Dicusses business methods, and how they are exposed 
to the client via the remote interface.
