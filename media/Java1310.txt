The first lesson in a miniseries intended to get 
you up and running with Sun's Enterprise JavaBeans(TM) 
very quickly.

Lists the specific steps required to get a bean up and 
running in Sun's J2EE environment.  Provides several 
explanations as to "What is an EJB?"  Discusses the purpose 
and goal of EJB, along with a very brief description as to 
how that goal is achieved.
