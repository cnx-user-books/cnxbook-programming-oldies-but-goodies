The lesson begins with a clarification of the difference between JavaBean Components(TM) and Enterprise JavaBeans(TM), along with a definition of enterprise beans

This is followed by a discussion of the Java 2 Platform, Enterprise Edition (J2EE).

The lesson discusses multi-tier systems, fat clients, and thin clients and gives gives some examples of why multi-tier systems with thin clients may be preferable to the use of two-tier systems with fat 
clients.

The need for standardization in multi-tier systems is discussed along with Sun's approach to the needed standardization.
