This is the first lesson in a new series on EJB.

This lesson introduce enterprise data processing, Enterprise JavaBeans(TM), and Sun's Java 2 Platform
Enterprise Edition. 

It explains the difference between Enterprise JavaBeans(TM) and JavaBean Components(TM). 

It briefly ventures into multi-tier systems, which will be more fully explained in future lessons.

Real-world examples of multi-tier systems are discussed.

This lesson is "must" reading for those who will want to pursue the entire series on EJB.
